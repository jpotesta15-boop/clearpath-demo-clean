# Client Persona Review

*Written as if by a client using the portal to view offers, pay for sessions, and schedule with their coach.*

---

## What I Love

- **Clear Schedule page.** I see my session offers in one place: package name, amount in dollars, and what to do next (Accept and pay, or Submit availability after paying). The flow is easy to follow.

- **Accept and pay.** One button takes me to Stripe Checkout; after I pay, I'm back on Schedule and can submit when I'm available. No guessing.

- **Submit availability.** I can type when I'm free (e.g. Mornings next week, Tue 2pm) and submit. My coach then picks a time and it shows up under My Sessions as confirmed.

- **Decline option.** If I can't take an offer right now, I can decline instead of leaving it hanging. I like that my coach will see Declined so they're not waiting on me.

- **My Sessions.** I see upcoming and past sessions with status (pending, confirmed) and date/time. Simple and clear.

- **Available Time Slots.** I can see when my coach is free and request a session from a slot if I want to book without going through an offer first.

- **Dashboard and Programs.** Dashboard shows upcoming sessions and my programs; Programs lists what my coach has assigned. Everything is in one place.

---

## Friction / What Was Missing

- **No way to decline (fixed).** Before, I could only accept or ignore an offer. Now I can decline so my coach knows I'm not interested this time.

- **Empty states.** Some sections show "No sessions scheduled" or "No available slots" with no next step. A short line like "Your coach will add availability" or "Accept an offer above to get started" would help.

- **Layout consistency.** Some pages feel denser than others; cards and spacing could be more uniform so the whole portal feels like one system.

- **Loading.** "Loading..." is fine but a subtle skeleton or fade-in when content appears would make the app feel more polished.

---

## Wishlist

- **Decline button (done).** So I can politely decline an offer and my coach sees it.

- **Clear CTAs.** Every empty or next step state should have a clear action (e.g. Accept an offer above, Submit when you're available).

- **Consistent cards and spacing.** Same card style and padding on Dashboard, Schedule, Programs, Videos so the client side feels cohesive.

- **Optional: pick my time.** After I pay, it would be nice to sometimes choose from my coach's available slots instead of only typing when I'm free. Then coach just confirms.

- **Light animations.** Cards or lists fading in on load would make the portal feel more responsive and sharp.

---

## Summary

The client flow works well: see offers, Accept and pay or Decline, after payment submit availability, coach picks time, session appears in My Sessions. The addition of Decline and clear statuses improves the experience. Next: empty-state copy, consistent formatting, and light load animations so the UI feels complete and polished.
